## Strategy & Vision

### Product Vision Development
```
Act as a strategic product leader. Help me articulate a compelling product vision for [PRODUCT NAME] that:
- Addresses a significant market opportunity in [MARKET/CATEGORY]
- Differentiates from competitors through [KEY DIFFERENTIATORS]
- Resonates with our target users: [USER PERSONA]
- Aligns with our company mission: [COMPANY MISSION]
- Can be communicated in 2-3 sentences

Provide:
- Vision statement (2-3 sentences)
- Key strategic pillars (3-5 pillars)
- Success metrics (leading and lagging indicators)
- 5-year product evolution roadmap
- How this vision creates sustainable competitive advantage
```

### OKR Development
```
I'm setting OKRs for [PRODUCT/TEAM] for [QUARTER/YEAR]. Help me create:
- 3-5 Objectives that are ambitious yet achievable (70% confidence)
- Key Results that are measurable, time-bound, and outcome-focused
- Success criteria that align with our product strategy: [STRATEGY CONTEXT]

Current context: [CURRENT STATE, CHALLENGES, OPPORTUNITIES]

For each Objective, provide:
- Objective statement (qualitative, inspirational)
- 2-4 Key Results (quantitative, measurable)
- Baseline metrics
- Target metrics
- Confidence level
- Dependencies and risks
- How to track progress weekly/monthly
```

Analyze the market opportunity for [PRODUCT IDEA/FEATURE] in [MARKET SEGMENT]:

- Revenue potential and business model considerations

Provide:

## Feature Prioritization

### RICE Scoring
```
Calculate RICE scores for these features:

[FEATURE LIST WITH CONTEXT]

For each feature, help me determine:
- Reach: [HOW MANY USERS IMPACTED PER QUARTER]
- Impact: [IMPACT LEVEL: 0.25/0.5/1/2/3]
- Confidence: [CONFIDENCE %: 50/80/100]
- Effort: [PERSON-MONTHS]

RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

Provide:
- Scored prioritization with rationale
- Sensitivity analysis (what if estimates are off?)
- Confidence intervals
- Recommendations with risk assessment
- Alternative prioritization scenarios
```

### ICE Scoring (Impact, Confidence, Ease)
```
Calculate ICE scores for rapid prioritization:

[FEATURE LIST]

For each feature, score 1-10:
- Impact: [USER/BUSINESS IMPACT: 1-10]
- Confidence: [CONFIDENCE IN IMPACT: 1-10]
- Ease: [EASE OF IMPLEMENTATION: 1-10]

ICE Score = (Impact + Confidence + Ease) / 3

Provide:
- Prioritized list with scores
- Quick wins identification (high impact, high ease)
- Strategic bets (high impact, lower ease)
- Risk mitigation for high-confidence items
```

### Kano Model Analysis

## Usage Tips

### Getting Started
1. **Customize Context**: Replace bracketed placeholders `[LIKE THIS]` with your specific context
2. **Start Simple**: Begin with basic prompts, then layer in complexity
3. **Iterate**: Use prompts iteratively - refine based on initial outputs
4. **Combine**: Mix prompts from different sections for comprehensive analysis
5. **Adapt**: Modify prompts to fit your organization's frameworks and terminology

### Best Practices
6. **Document**: Save your customized prompts and results for future reference
7. **Share**: Build a team library of effective prompts
8. **Version Control**: Track which prompts work best for different scenarios
9. **Context is Key**: Provide as much relevant context as possible for better outputs
10. **Review & Refine**: Always review AI outputs critically and refine based on your expertise

### Advanced Usage
11. **Chain Prompts**: Use output from one prompt as input to another for deeper analysis
12. **A/B Test Prompts**: Try different phrasings to see what works best
13. **Template Library**: Create your own prompt templates for recurring tasks
14. **Collaboration**: Use prompts in team meetings to facilitate discussions
15. **Learning Tool**: Use prompts to learn new frameworks and methodologies

### Prompt Engineering Tips
- **Be Specific**: More specific prompts yield better results
- **Provide Examples**: Include examples of desired output format
- **Set Constraints**: Specify length, format, or style requirements
- **Ask for Rationale**: Request explanations to understand reasoning
- **Request Alternatives**: Ask for multiple options when appropriate
